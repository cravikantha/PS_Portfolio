Chapter 10 – Negotiation Skills

Content

This chapter was about how to talk and agree in smart way. In jobs and life, we need to negotiate for things like salary, deals, or solving problems.

We learned about two types: distributive (win-lose) and integrative (win-win). We saw steps of negotiation and tools like BATNA (Best Option if talk fails). Also, we learned how to stay calm and talk nice.

What I Learnt

  • Steps and rules of negotiation.
  • Why being ready is important.
  • How to talk and compromise.
  • How to control emotions in talks.
  • Where to use negotiation in real life.

What Was Interesting

I liked how being ready before talking makes a big difference. Role-play was fun and taught me how to stay calm and think fast. I saw that negotiation is not just about winning, but about helping both sides.
