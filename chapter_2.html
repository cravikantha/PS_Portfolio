Chapter 02 – Portfolio Management

Content

Portfolios are used in many ways. For job, they show your work and skills to companies. They help in career growth too. In creative jobs, like artist or writer, portfolios show style and talent. Students use them to track what they learn. Portfolios also help in getting into schools or colleges. Teachers show their teaching growth with it. Freelancers use them to get clients by showing past work.

They also help show professional growth like courses or new skills. Bosses use them to check performance. Entrepreneurs use portfolios to show plans to investors. It’s also good for networking and personal brand.

There are many kinds: personal, career, work, and study portfolios. Websites like LinkedIn, Behance, and Google Sites help in making online portfolios.

What I Learnt

  • Portfolios help in jobs, promotions, and showing skills.
  • Artists and freelancers use them to show their work.
  • Students use them to track what they learn.
  • Shows growth like new skills or courses.
  • Entrepreneurs use them to show ideas to investors.
  • Many types: personal, career, job, and study.
  • Sites like LinkedIn and WordPress help make portfolios.
  • Good for networking and building image.

What Was Interesting

I didn’t know portfolios could be used in so many ways – not just for jobs but also for school and creative work. Freelancers using them to get clients was new to me. I also liked how they help show your growth, like new things you learn. It’s nice how sites like LinkedIn make it easy to build one.
