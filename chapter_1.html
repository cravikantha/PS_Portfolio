Chapter 01 – Introduction to Professional Skills

Content

Professional skills, also called soft skills, are non-technical things that help in doing a job well. These skills go with technical knowledge to make a person more helpful at work. They work in many jobs. Like speaking clearly, working in teams, solving problems, thinking smart, using time well, and leading others. There are three kinds of work skills – technical, soft, and transferable. Values, beliefs, attitude, and character also affect how we act. Values help us choose right things. Beliefs change how we see the world. Attitude change our behavior. Character shows if someone is honest or strong. The Johari Window helps people know about themselves and others better. Learning these skills help us get jobs, work better, and grow in career. To improve, we should keep learning, use skills daily, take feedback, and try new things.

What I Learnt

  • Soft skills matter – They help in getting jobs and career growth.
  • Different skills – Technical (job), soft (people), transferable (any job).
  • Johari Window – A tool to know self and talk better.
  • Communication, teamwork & leadership – Helps in success.
  • How to grow soft skills – Keep learning and take advice.
