Chapter 09 – Research Design

Content

This lesson taught how to plan and do research. It’s helpful for projects in school or job. Research design means how we collect, study, and explain data.

We learned about types of research – qualitative, quantitative, and both. We saw methods like surveys, interviews, and experiments. It also told us to define the problem, choose right sample, and follow rules.

What I Learnt

  • What makes a strong research plan.
  • Different kinds of research.
  • How to write good questions.
  • How to pick people and collect data.
  • Rules and fairness in research.

What Was Interesting

I liked learning about bias and how it changes results. Even one wrong word in question can give wrong answers. I liked learning how to plan step-by-step research.
